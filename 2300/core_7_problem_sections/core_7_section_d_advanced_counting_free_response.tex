\section*{D}
\begin{enumerate}
\item
How many different strings can be made by reordering the letters of the word \texttt{BANANA}?

\item
How many ways are there to distribute 52 cards into 3 hands of 5 cards if the three hands are distinguishable?


\item
There are three tip jars, one labeled \texttt{"Team Jacob"} and another labeled \texttt{"Team Edward"}
and a third jar labeled \texttt{"Team Bella"}.
How many ways can \$100 be distributed between the three jars?
\item
How many ways are there to place 10 indistinguishable balls into 5 distinguishable boxes?

\end{enumerate}
